Octatrack MK2

I mainly use the Octatrack as a if it was a desktop synth. This is where I mangle audio and devolop a lot of my ideas. I usually sync this with the E-RM multi clock so I can record phrases into my daw. I find this to be one of the most inspiring machines in my setup. not the easiest pieces of gear to work with but totally worth the results.
