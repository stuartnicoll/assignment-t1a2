Quantum

The Waldorf Quantum is a digital poly synth and is my main lady in the studio. I make most of my sounds here as it is such a flexible machine and sounds amazing. She has 3 multi mode oscillators with two analog filters, six envelopes and four lfos. Plus it is dual tambral with 8 voice polyphony. I will never leave her!
